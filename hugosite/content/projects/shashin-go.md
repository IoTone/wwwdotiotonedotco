## What it is

Shashin GO is an open-source DIY wearable AI camera platform. It captures your most important moments without missing them, while signaling privacy intent (privacy tags / `norobots`) so AI scrapers should leave private data alone.

## Concepts shipped

- **Shashin GO Concept 1** — first reference design.
- **Shashin GO Concept 2** — refined wearable form factor.

Both were demoed publicly at **CES 2024 in Las Vegas**.

Shipped — hardware files + firmware are public. Limited DIY production + OSS coming. Contact us for sales.
