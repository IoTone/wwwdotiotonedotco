## What it is

RockeTone is IoTone Japan's project to modernize papercraft and model rockets with IoT hardware and software — sensors, telemetry, and post-flight data, packaged for STEM and maker audiences.

> プロジェクトは紙飛行機と小型ロケットをIOTによって最新化する。

## Status

Active. Running as both an OSS reference and a Japan-localized educational program.
