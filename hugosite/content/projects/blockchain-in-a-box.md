Blockchain-In-A-Box was designed for the rapid prototyping of enterprise-oriented blockchain solutions and Industrial / IoT blockchain dapps. We integrated mobile applications that worked "out of the box" with minimal configuration, on Android and iOS, deployable to public or private blockchain networks.

While the big tech companies wanted vendor lock-in, IoTone's offer was a completely open solution for development and deployment.
