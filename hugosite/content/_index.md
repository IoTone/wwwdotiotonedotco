## A small studio with a long memory.

IoTone, Inc. has been quietly building an open-source ecosystem of wearable, spatial, and IoT devices since 2014 — long enough to remember Zigbee 1.0, MFi, the original Vision Pro reveal, and the first Snap Spectacles. We work where platforms fail: bridging APIs, polyfilling missing primitives, and shipping reference hardware others can build on.

Started in Silicon Valley in 2014; today the studio is headquartered in PDX (Portland, OR) with ongoing operations in Fukuoka, Japan. We sponsor open source. We work for hire through IoTone Japan.
