IoTone, Inc. is founded in Silicon Valley.

The original thesis: a single platform — *Unified Device Intelligence* — that puts AI on cloud, desktop, mobile, and IoT, with privacy and developer time as first-order concerns. We called the platform *BiotKit*. For developers, it saves time and simplifies deployment. For device makers, it reduces time to market. For consumers, it means interacting with devices and the world more naturally — fewer swipes, clicks, and confirmation dialogs.

In 2014, those were ambitious words. A decade later, with [Project MAG*Net](https://github.com/IoTone/ProjectMagNET), [Polynode](https://github.com/IoTone/Spectacles-polynode), [offlinesns](https://github.com/IoTone/offlinesns), and 40+ other public projects, the thesis is roughly intact — and a lot of the platforms have changed names.
