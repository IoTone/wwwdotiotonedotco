[ShashinGo](https://github.com/IoTone/ShashinGo) is the Shashin Go Concept 1 + 2 — open-source hardware files for a DIY wearable AI-data-capture camera. The platform is designed to capture moments without missing them, while signaling privacy intent (privacy tags / norobots) so AI scrapers should leave private data alone.

Demoed publicly at CES 2024 in Las Vegas. Limited DIY production + OSS coming.
