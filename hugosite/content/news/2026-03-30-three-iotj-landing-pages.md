Three IoTone Japan side-projects each get a landing page:

- **RobotARme** — [robotarme-www](https://github.com/IoTone/robotarme-www): platform landing.
- **Moire Lens Lab** — [www-moirelenslab](https://github.com/IoTone/www-moirelenslab) at moirelenslab.iotj.cc: an experimental art-book project that uses [Polynode](https://github.com/IoTone/Spectacles-polynode)'s SpaceSVG.
- **No Foosball No Life** — [nofoosballnolife-www](https://github.com/IoTone/nofoosballnolife-www) for the SpectaclesFoosball community + issue tracker.
