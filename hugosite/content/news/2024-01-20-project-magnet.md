[Project MAG*Net](https://github.com/IoTone/ProjectMagNET) is the spiritual successor to IoToneKit. It's an IoT data sync and control app + a distributed server, designed to span Matter, Zigbee, Thread, HomeKit, BLE, and Wi-Fi without locking developers into a single platform. C++ + ESP32 + Arduino tooling.
