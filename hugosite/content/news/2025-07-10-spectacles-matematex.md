[Spectacles-Matematex](https://github.com/IoTone/Spectacles-Matematex) parses and renders LaTex math notation on Snap Spectacles. The use case that drove the [Polynode](https://github.com/IoTone/Spectacles-polynode) project — Matematex needs SpaceSVG to render output cleanly.
