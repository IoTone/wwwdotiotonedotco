[cl-abcl-gradle-bootstrap](https://github.com/IoTone/cl-abcl-gradle-bootstrap) wraps ABCL (Armed Bear Common Lisp) with a Gradle build so you can ship Common Lisp services on the JVM with the same devops tooling as everything else (Netty, Gradle wrappers, CI).
