[SpectaclesDeskWindow](https://github.com/IoTone/SpectaclesDeskWindow), created by IoTone Japan, is a mirroring solution for the Snap Spectacles AR glasses. It's the first OSS solution for remotely viewing your desktop (or a desktop-attached camera) in a spatial context on Spectacles. Source is on GitHub.
